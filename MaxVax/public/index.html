Vaccine Fast Facts

  • The COVID-19 vaccine helps the body build immunity to the virus.
  • There are currently 2 types of vaccines available in the US.
  • mRNA vaccines give our cells instructions to make harmless proteins unique to the virus, which they fight off and remember, the body's immune response.
  • Vector vaccines deliver a modified version of a virus, containing a material that causes COVID.
  • Using this information, our cells create proteins, and similar to the mRNA vaccine, our body uses this to remember how to combat the virus.

Why Should I Get Vaccinated?

Your parents. Your siblings. Your grandparents and relatives. Your best friends, coworkers, and significant other. Anyone you see on a daily basis. These are only a fraction of the people you are putting at risk by being unprotected against COVID-19. Being vaccinated can help protect all the people you love, and help life get back to normal.
